BOOKING & GOOD KARMA




Whenever you feel a connection with our work, or simply appreciate the craft behind it, we warmly invite you to get in touch for your next project.

At Riviera Moretti we approach every collaboration with full commitment. In order to maintain fairness and professional integrity, we do not accept commissions from different teams competing on the same project or competition.

Good karma begins with good planning.

We strongly recommend booking projects several weeks in advance and respecting the timeline agreed together. This allows us to organise our schedule properly and ensure the level of care and quality each project deserves.

During the working process we aim to remain flexible and solution-oriented. Architecture and design projects often evolve, and we do our best to adapt when reasonable adjustments are required. That said, all work is carried out within the scope, timeline and conditions previously agreed.

The agreed schedule always depends on the timely delivery of all necessary materials, information, and feedback from the client’s side.
If required materials, approvals, or feedback are delayed after the project has been confirmed, the delivery schedule may need to be adjusted accordingly.
We will always do our best to accommodate changes to the working period or deadlines when possible. However, this may lead to overlaps with other scheduled work — which, from a karma perspective, is never ideal.

If delays or changes make the originally agreed timeline unfeasible, Riviera Moretti reserves the right to reschedule the delivery or, if necessary, suspend or terminate the collaboration. In such cases Riviera Moretti cannot be held responsible for delays caused by missing materials, postponed approvals, or changes outside our control.

From time to time we may be able to assist with last-minute or emergency requests. Feel free to reach out if you find yourself in a tight situation — we understand that architecture deadlines can occasionally become dramatic. However, emergency availability cannot be guaranteed and should never be assumed.

A project is considered officially booked only once the scope of work, timeline and financial terms have been clearly agreed and confirmed in writing (email confirmation is sufficient). Until such confirmation has been received, Riviera Moretti cannot be considered formally engaged in the project.

If no confirmation or response is received after an offer has been issued, we will assume that the project will not proceed and our availability may no longer be guaranteed.

Clear communication, realistic timelines and mutual respect always lead to the good results.

Thank you for your interest.
We will review your request and get back to you shortly.
