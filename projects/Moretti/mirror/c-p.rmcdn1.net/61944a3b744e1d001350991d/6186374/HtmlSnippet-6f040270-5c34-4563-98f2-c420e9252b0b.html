RIVIERA MORETTI

Riviera Moretti is an independent studio based in Vienna, specialising in art-directed illustrations for concept-driven architecture, competitions, and pitch entries. Our work explores a painterly and atmospheric approach to visual storytelling, seeking to capture space, lingering light, and narrative beyond literal photorealism. Over the years, our images have contributed to numerous award-winning projects and recognised proposals in both Austrian and international competitions. In a few words, this is what we do here.
