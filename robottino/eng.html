Robottino

interactive and programmable

Plug an Arduino board on its back, then set the instructions and combining components: a small display, sensors, sounds, coloured lights and a little motor to spin the head.
Has Internet access and works with your web-applications.
For kids, students and Arduino enthusiasts.

AMBIENT SENSORS

feels the ambient: eyes for measuring distances from objects, antennas for the intensity of light

ACTUATORS

all the actuators can be combined: coloured lights, sounds and spinning-head will respond to your programs

INTERNET OF THINGS

doesn't need any computer to work with web-applications: mail, facebook, twitter... and many others

MINY DISPLAY

the mouth can display values from the sensors, drawings and informations from the web



Make

DO IT YOURSELF
all you need to make a Robottino:
components list, design files, codes


Arduino boards and software

Robottino works and can be programmed with Arduino boards: Uno, Leonardo, Yùn (web connection) and many others.
The software is free and can be downloaded from the Arduino site.

The project

Robottino is still a prototype: we are working on design, circuits and developing software libraries, examples and tutorials to make programming as easy as possible.
Follow the project on Facebook or subscribe below to our list if you want to be informed when Robottino will be distributed.
