About the Life Weeks Grid

This grid represents the weeks of a human life. Each small cell corresponds to one week. The weeks that you have already lived are marked in black. This visual representation serves as a reminder of the time that has passed and the time that is estimated to remain, based on average life expectancies (80 years for males, 85 years for females). It's a powerful tool for reflection and planning, encouraging a mindful approach to how we spend our weeks.
